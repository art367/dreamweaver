## The Story

This was born from a simple wish: *I want something to help me sleep.* Not an app store download with subscriptions and upsells. Just a single page that dims the lights, speaks softly, and carries you into the deep.

Built entirely by AI in a single conversation. One file. No frameworks. No tracking. Just you and the dark.

## What It Does

A gentle female voice guides you through a hypnotic journey to sleep — from settling in, through a full body scan, down a moonlit staircase of deepening relaxation, into an enchanted garden, and finally into sleep itself.

The screen slowly dims as you go deeper. The ambient soundscape — ocean waves, deep drones, distant chimes — plays beneath the words like a warm blanket.

**Best with headphones, in a dark room.**

## The Journey

| Phase | Experience |
|-------|-----------|
| 🌿 Settling In | Deep breaths, releasing the day |
| ✋ Body Scan | Wave of warmth from forehead to toes |
| 🌙 Deepening | Descending a moonlit staircase, 10 to 1 |
| 🌺 Enchanted Garden | Fireflies, lavender, starlight |
| 💤 Drift to Sleep | Voice fades, darkness wraps around you |

## What's Inside

- Web Audio API ambient soundscape — layered ocean noise, harmonic drones, periodic chimes
- Speech Synthesis with automatic female voice selection
- Breathing mandala with animated petal geometry
- Progressive screen dimming that follows your descent
- Cosmic particle field with firefly pulsing
- Wake Lock API to prevent screen sleep
- Mobile + desktop support
